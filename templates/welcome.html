Our Story.

We are two UW student in the engineering department. This starts off as a project in HCDE 310 class. However, seeing that a lot of students are stuggling in their job searching process. We have decided to create this application. This eliminate the need for constant job hunting through various websites.

Job searching is unfiar, irrational, and you can't learn about it because no one is willing or able to teach you what it is all about. Although there are studdents who are confident in their ability to find a job and deal with the litany of emotions that surround a job search, these are rare exception Therefore we hope this search engine can benefit students who are going through this process.
